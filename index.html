ABOUT

DNB is an Architectural / Engineering company founded with 20 years of experience overseas and inland by a core team of professionals in the field of architecture, Interiors, Engineering and construction management.

DNB is a new Brand formed by strategic partnership of qualified professionals like an Architect, a Structural Engineer and a MEP Engineer, Formerly operating under different names SRI PADHMA ENGINEERS AND CONTRACTORS (SPEC) & ROCK DEVELOPMENT CONSORTIUM (RDC). Within less than 9 years of market presence we have successfully completed an appreciable numbers of industrial, commercial and residential projects for corporate and individual clients.
